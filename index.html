About Us

Congress Coffee

Congress Coffee is dedicated to sourcing the highest quality African green coffee beans through direct partnerships with farmers. We believe in ethical sourcing practices that support the livelihood of farmers and their communities. Our mission is to provide the UK market with exceptional coffee beans that are sustainably and responsibly sourced.

At Congress Coffee, we are passionate about bringing the exceptional flavors of African coffee to the world. We specialize in wholesaling green coffee beans that are sourced directly from farmers across the African continent.

Our mission is clear, to ethically source the finest coffee beans and create a positive impact in the communities where they are grown. We believe in building direct relationships with farmers, paying them a premium price that goes well above the market rate. By doing so, we ensure that our farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work and dedication.

We are committed to transparency and traceability in our sourcing process. With a deep understanding of the complexities of the coffee industry, we personally travel to coffee-growing regions, forging connections with farmers. This hands-on approach allows us to select only the highest quality beans and maintain a direct line of communication with our partners.
